About

Sarah Zachrich Jeng is originally from Michigan and grew up writing stories on her parents' old Smith-Corona typewriter. She had a brief career as an aspiring rock star, playing various instruments in more local bands than she can remember, until she realized she was terrible at waiting tables. So she went back to school, became a web developer, and (eventually) returned to writing. A University of Florida alum, Sarah lives in Gainesville, Florida with her multi-generational family and an extremely hyper rescue dog.
